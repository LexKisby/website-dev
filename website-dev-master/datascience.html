Data Science A core part of modern computing

Data science is a field that uses algorithms and systems to analyse data and determine patterns that can then be used to predict patterns and trends.

History of Data Science
Originally, in the 1960s, the term 'data science' was used in place of 'computer science.' A distinction between 'computer science' and 'data science' was first clearly noted in C.F Jeff Wu's inaugaral lecture, which was called "Statistics = Data Science?".
